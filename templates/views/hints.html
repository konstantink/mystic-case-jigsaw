Square

In the jigsaw, the houses are scattered. Consider them according to the numbering: from the first to the seventh.

All houses are multi-colored.

The first house is red, the second is orange, the third is yellow, the fourth is green, the fifth is blue, the sixth is indigo, the seventh is violet. What is the name of the area?

The answer is RAINBOW.
Baloons

Each of the three large balloons has a piece of "ornament".

Connect the lines of the "ornament" according to the height of the balloons.

The word is written on the balloons, divided into 3 parts. By connecting the lines from the top ball to the middle and bottom, you get the word.

The answer is CLOUD.
Excursion

Find all the hidden figures in the jigsaw.

Mentally (or on paper) connect the figures in the sequence indicated in the task.

Connected dots form a boat figure. Find the boat on the jigsaw.

The answer is NEPTUNE.
Souvenir products

On the jigsaw find people with flags, balloons, in caps, T-shirts, with umbrellas.

Write down the letters on each type of object (on flags, on balloons, etc.) and make 5 words out of them.

EXHIBITION, PAINTING, HISTORY, GUIDE, ART - identify the common for these words and name the town institution that distributes souvenirs.

The answer is MUSEUM.
Ticket

The shape of the wooden ticket exactly matches the four objects on the jigsaw.

Attach the ticket to 4 suitable objects and see which symbol is visible in the hole.

Attach a wooden ticket to the poster so that one arrow points to the received character, then the second one points to the required letter. Put together the 4-letter password.

The answer is VIEW.
